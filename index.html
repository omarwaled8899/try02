Copart Select Grading Criteria

Clean Title

WCopart Select inventory only includes clean titles, which are typically drivable and insurable. They include no salvage titles, junk titles, reconstructed or rebuilt titles.

Read More

Limited Damage

For Copart Select, we are only including vehicles with the following types of damage:

  • Minor dents and scratches
  • Normal wear and tear
  • Hail damage
  • Minor mechanical flaw
  • Minor front- and rear-end damage
Read More

Lower Mileage

On average, Copart Select vehicles will have around 65,000 miles, and will typically not exceed 150,000 miles.

Read More

Late Model

On average, Copart Select vehicles will be around 5 to 6 years old, and typically no older than 8 years.
